Simcoe Geoscience Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how Simcoe Geoscience handles user information and ensures confidentiality. All information provided and any files transmitted with it are intended solely for the use of Simcoe Geoscience in assisting users to learn about or acquire geophysical survey services.

Confidentiality

Simcoe Geoscience treats all information provided as confidential. This information is protected and will not be distributed to any third party. Our privacy policy strictly prohibits disclosing, copying, or distributing any information provided through LinkedIn, email, phone, teleconferencing, or other sources.

Who We Are and What This Policy Covers

Simcoe Geoscience provides geophysical survey services and consulting. This privacy policy applies to all data and information acquired from clients, ensuring strict adherence to confidentiality and data protection principles.

What Personal Information We Collect

  • User-provided information, such as name, contact details, and project-related data, to assist with geophysical survey services.
  • Files or documentation shared via email, phone, teleconferencing, or other means for service purposes.
  • Any additional data required to fulfill our commitment to delivering high-quality geoscience services.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or how your information is handled, please Contact Us.
