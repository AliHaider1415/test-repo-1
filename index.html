Geotechnical Investigations

Geotechnical investigations use modern geophysical methods to assess subsurface conditions, ensuring site stability, infrastructure safety, and risk reduction.

Environmental Investigations

Environmental investigations assess contamination risks and ensure compliance with regulations, promoting sustainability and public safety.

Exploration Services

Exploration services use advanced geophysical methods to locate deposits beneath thick overburden and at greater depths. Simcoe Geoscience offers tailored solutions for projects of any size.

Engineering Services

Engineering services provide geophysical solutions for site assessments, infrastructure planning, and subsurface investigations, ensuring safety and efficiency for projects of all sizes.

Consulting (Ground & Airborne)

Consulting services offer expert ground and airborne geophysical solutions, providing insights for exploration, site analysis, and project planning.

Geological Studies
